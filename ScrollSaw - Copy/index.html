Making things with a scroll saw should be accessible to everyone.
Mayberry ScrollSaw was created to help hobbyists of all skill levels design, create, and build projects they can be proud of. Our goal is simple: bring modern technology into the scroll sawing hobby without replacing the craftsmanship that makes it special. Whether you're cutting your first pattern or designing your own, you'll find tools, patterns, and resources built specifically for scroll saw makers—not CNC machines or lasers. We're here to help more people discover, enjoy, and share the craft of scroll sawing. Welcome to the workshop.

About the Shop


"A piece of wood, a simple pattern, and a scroll saw — that's all it takes."

Woodworking can feel expensive and intimidating when you're starting out. We're working to change that with free tools that let anyone design their own patterns — whether they've been scroll sawing for twenty years or just unboxed their first saw.

The lessons a scroll saw teaches — patience, creativity, problem solving, pride in a finished project — are worth passing on. That's the Mayberry way.
